# Nova Tone — Creative Brief

## Hook
**Nova Tone** is a premium analog-style tone EQ inspired by the musical philosophy of the Pultec EQP-1A — designed to add weight, gloss, air, and polish with broad, flattering curves rather than surgical correction.

## Vision
Nova Tone belongs to the same family as `Space By Nova`, but with its own identity: boutique mastering hardware energy, warm ivory metalwork, and rich gold control accents. The goal is for users to open it and immediately feel like they are working with a real piece of studio outboard gear.

## Inspiration Reference
The tonal philosophy should draw from:
- **Pultec EQP-1A**
- **Waves PuigTec**
- **Universal Audio Pultec Collection**

The key emotional target is the classic Pultec behavior: broad, flattering curves, low-end weight with simultaneous boost/attenuation, and a polished top end that feels expensive rather than hyped.

## Sonic Goal
- Prioritize **musical tone shaping** over technical precision
- Emphasize **broad, smooth, forgiving EQ curves**
- Allow the classic low-end behavior of **boosting and attenuating the same frequency simultaneously**
- Add a subtle analog-style finish so the plugin feels polished, rounded, and expensive
- Avoid harsh digital edges or brittle top end

## Target Use Cases
- **Vocals:** sweet presence, top-end air, and polished body
- **Bass:** fuller fundamentals with controlled thickness
- **Air:** glossy upper lift without sharpness
- **Master:** gentle final sweetening and broad tonal enhancement

## Control Philosophy
Keep the interface simple and intuitive, with clearly separated sections:
- **Low Section** for weight and warmth
- **High Boost Section** for presence and sheen
- **High Attenuation Section** for taming edge smoothly
- **Output Gain** for level matching after tone shaping
- **Mode Buttons** for instant musical starting points

## Preset Mode Personalities
- **Vocal:** polished presence and flattering top-end lift
- **Bass:** enhanced low-end body with restrained highs
- **Air:** silky brightness and open top
- **Master:** subtle broad-stroke sweetening for buses or mix finishing

## Visual Direction
Use a premium hardware-inspired look with a warm champagne palette:
- Main background around `#F2E8DC`
- Slightly darker inner panel around `#E4D6C3`
- Soft top highlight and gentle bottom shadow for metal depth
- Labels in a warm dark brown like `#4A4036`
- Gold controls that feel tactile and rich, but not identical to `Space By Nova`

## UX Notes
- One-screen layout
- Obvious section labeling
- Large, confidence-inspiring controls
- Analog hardware feel, not generic software utility
- Default state should be visually elegant and sonically neutral
