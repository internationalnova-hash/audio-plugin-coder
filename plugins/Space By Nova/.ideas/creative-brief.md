# Space By Nova — Creative Brief

## Hook
**Space By Nova** is a premium vocal-first reverb designed to make vocals feel expensive, wide, and emotionally cinematic without washing out the source. It targets the polished, modern ambience heard across R&B, Latin, pop, and melodic rap.

## Vision
Create a reverb that feels **luxurious**, **spacious**, **smooth**, and **musical** from the first knob turn. The plugin should deliver fast results for producers who want a high-end sound without navigating a dense technical panel.

## Sonic Character
- **Smooth tail** with no harsh top-end
- **Modern stereo width** for wide, premium vocals
- **Clean vocal clarity** that preserves intelligibility
- **Slight analog warmth** for a polished, expensive finish
- Friendly for styles inspired by **Drake**, **The Weeknd**, and **Bad Bunny**

## Product Positioning
Space By Nova should feel like a boutique studio tool:
- Easy enough for beginners
- Powerful enough for professional vocal chains
- Premium enough to feel like luxury hardware inside a DAW

## Core Experience
The front panel should stay simple and musical, centered on five macro controls:
- **Space** — makes the room larger and more immersive
- **Air** — adds shimmer and top-end openness
- **Depth** — pushes the vocal further into the reverb field
- **Mix** — controls wet/dry balance
- **Width** — expands stereo spread

A dedicated **Nova Mode** switch provides instant signature flavors:
- **Studio** — tight, clear, vocal-forward
- **Arena** — large, lush, modern, emotional
- **Dream** — ambient, floaty, cinematic
- **Vintage** — warm, plate-like, musical

## Visual Direction
The plugin should feel premium and instantly recognizable as part of the Nova brand.

### Primary look
- **Matte white base**
- **Gold knobs** with subtle premium highlights
- **Light gray accents**
- **Soft shadows and restrained glow**
- **Minimal control count and clean spacing**

### Brand references
- Luxury hardware feel similar to **Universal Audio** and **Softube**
- Clean, modern presentation with boutique studio confidence

## Suggested Default Use Cases
- Lead vocals
- Rap vocals
- Pop hooks
- Adlibs
- Background vocals
- Ambient pads and intros/outros

## Signature Identity
**Nova Mode** is the product’s “secret sauce” identity. Rather than exposing many technical parameters by default, Space By Nova should feel fast, musical, and confidence-inspiring.

Mode changes should also feel **premium in motion**: when users switch presets, the visible controls should glide smoothly into place over roughly **100–300 ms** instead of snapping abruptly.

## Future Brand Potential
This concept can grow into a matching suite such as:
- **Nova Heat** — saturation / tone shaping
- **Nova Tone** — EQ / polish
- **Nova Suite** / **International Nova Audio** branding direction
