# Nova Space

## Hook

Nova Space is a vocal-first space reverb that turns a handful of musical controls into polished, expensive-sounding depth. It is designed to stand out instantly in a DAW, both visually and sonically: bright white interface, premium gold hardware feel, and a reverb character that moves from tight studio gloss to wide cinematic atmosphere without exposing technical clutter.

## Description

Nova Space is aimed at producers who want a modern vocal reverb they can dial in quickly. The default sound should feel smooth, slightly warm, wide, and clean enough to preserve lyric intelligibility while still adding size and air. Its core design principle is simplification through musical macro controls:

- `Space` makes the room feel bigger by combining room size, decay behavior, and reflection spread.
- `Depth` changes how far the source sits back by blending pre-delay behavior with tail presence and wet emphasis.
- `Air` brightens or darkens the reverb tail for modern pop sheen or warmer vintage softness.
- `Width` expands the stereo field from focused mono ambience to wide cinematic spread.
- `Mix` provides the final wet/dry balance.

The signature feature is `Nova Mode`, which switches the algorithm personality without requiring the user to understand reverb topology:

- `Studio`: tight, clean, vocal-forward ambience.
- `Arena`: large, modern, wide hall space.
- `Dream`: long, airy, soft ambient wash.
- `Vintage`: warm plate-inspired reverb with analog character.

The product position is premium but accessible. It should feel equally useful on Latin, R&B, and pop vocals, while remaining attractive for pads, ambient production, and background textures. The UI direction is a bright white panel with gold controls and a centered hero width control, making the plugin instantly recognizable inside dark DAW environments.
