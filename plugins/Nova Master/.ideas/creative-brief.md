# Nova Master — Creative Brief

## Hook
**Nova Master** is the final mix processor for producers who want a record-ready finish without opening a full mastering suite.

It is the plugin you place on the mix bus when you want the track to feel:
- more polished
- more connected
- more expensive
- more finished

## Core Concept
Nova Master should not feel technical or surgical.
It should feel like a **premium finishing box** that combines several subtle mastering moves into one elegant workflow.

If:
- `Nova Tone` = shaping
- `Nova Heat` = color
- `Nova Level` = control

then:
- `Nova Master` = **finish**

## Sonic Goal
Nova Master should improve a mix through a few restrained, musical processes:
- tonal balance
- glue compression
- low-end foundation
- high-end openness
- stereo polish
- final output control

The result should be:
> **“the mix sounds more like a record.”**

## Core Differentiation
Nova Master should not compete by being louder, brighter, or more aggressive.

It should win by being:
- smoother
- more controlled
- more refined
- more finished

### One-line positioning
> **Nova Master doesn’t hype your mix — it finishes it.**

If many “finalizer” plugins are about immediate excitement, Nova Master should be about:
> **completion**

## User Experience Goal
The plugin should feel fast and confidence-inspiring:
1. place it on the mix bus
2. turn a few big knobs
3. pick a finishing mode
4. optionally engage `FINISH`
5. the mix gets better without needing technical mastering knowledge

## Main Controls
### 1. `Tone`
Overall tonal tilt from darker/smoother to brighter/more present.

### 2. `Glue`
Controls how connected and finished the mix feels through subtle bus compression.

### 3. `Weight`
Adds controlled low-end body and foundation without mud.

### 4. `Air`
Adds top-end openness, lift, and polish without harshness.

### 5. `Width`
Adds stereo polish and space while keeping the low end centered and stable.

## Secondary / Special Control
### `Finish`
A premium enhancement button that applies a final layer of smoothing, harmonic polish, stereo refinement, and output balancing.

It should feel like:
> **“the mix got more expensive.”**

## Mode Buttons
Nova Master should include four finishing personalities:
- `Clean`
- `Warm`
- `Wide`
- `Loud`

These should act as fast mix-bus voicings that instantly reposition the plugin toward a musical outcome.

## Visual Direction
Nova Master should feel like the flagship of the suite.

### UI character
- premium
- elegant
- restrained
- wide and balanced
- clearly mix-bus oriented

### Color direction
Use the Nova family champagne language, but make it slightly more refined:
- champagne base
- subtle platinum-gold accents
- cleaner and more elevated than Nova Heat
- more “mastering room” than “color box”

## North Star
Nova Master should feel like:
> **“What I put on the mix bus when I want it to sound like a finished record.”**
