# Nova Heat — Creative Brief

## Hook
**Nova Heat** is a premium harmonic saturation engine designed to add warmth, presence, glue, and energy to vocals, instruments, and full mixes without destroying the original signal.

## Vision
Nova Heat completes the core Nova family:
- **Nova Tone** → shaping
- **Space By Nova** → space
- **Nova Heat** → character

The experience should feel immediate and musical: a few large controls, strong results, and a polished boutique look that encourages creative use rather than technical overthinking.

## Sonic Goal
- Add **analog warmth and harmonic energy**
- Enhance **vocals and mix buses** without turning harsh or brittle
- Offer both **subtle polish** and **heavier color** from the same interface
- Preserve clarity through **parallel blend** and output compensation

## Core Character
Nova Heat should feel like:
- subtle tube warmth at low settings
- richer harmonic lift as Drive increases
- more attitude and edge as Heat increases
- tonal balance control through a bright/dark tilt response

The result should remain flattering and mix-friendly, not like a one-note fuzz box.

## Target Use Cases
- **Vocal Warmth:** body, density, and confidence
- **Vocal Presence:** forwardness and sheen without sharpness
- **Analog Glue:** bus cohesion and harmonic density
- **Tape Color:** softened transients and richer texture
- **Master Shine:** tasteful harmonic finishing on a stereo mix

## Control Philosophy
Keep the interface simple and pro-focused:
- **Drive** = amount of saturation
- **Tone** = bright ↔ dark saturation balance
- **Heat** = signature character and curve intensity
- **Mix** = parallel blend for controlled enhancement
- **Output** = gain compensation
- **Character Switches** = Tube / Tape / Analog / Digital voicings

## Visual Direction
Match the Nova family aesthetic, especially Nova Tone:
- warm champagne faceplate
- rich gold tactile knobs
- clearly divided panel sections
- premium mastering-hardware vibe
- modern clean layout with boutique polish

## Suggested Subtitle
- **Harmonic Saturation Engine**
- or **Analog Color & Drive**

## UX Notes
- One-page layout
- Large primary knobs with obvious readouts
- Bottom preset buttons for fast creative starting points
- Real-time output indication for level awareness
- Friendly for both vocals and mix-bus enhancement
