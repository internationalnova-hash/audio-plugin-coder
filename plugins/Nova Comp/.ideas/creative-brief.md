# Nova Comp — Creative Brief

## Hook
**Nova Comp** is a premium optical-style compressor inspired by the musical feel of the classic LA-2A, reimagined as a modern **Nova** product. It is designed to deliver instant leveling, warmth, and polish with a minimal control set and a boutique visual identity.

## Vision
This is **not** a clone. Nova Comp should keep the *fast setup, forgiving behavior, and smooth musical response* people love in optical compression, while adding a few carefully chosen Nova upgrades:

- **LEVEL** for fast compression amount
- **GAIN** for makeup gain
- **MIX** for built-in parallel compression
- **TONE** for dark-to-present tonal balance
- **MODE** switch for Smooth / Punch / Limit
- **MAGIC** for subtle smart enhancement

## Core Philosophy
> **Set it fast. Sounds good instantly.**

Nova Comp should feel effortless on vocals, bass, drums, and even gentle mix-bus glue. It should be the "control" piece of the Nova ecosystem:

- `Nova Space` = depth
- `Nova Tone` = shaping
- `Nova Heat` = energy
- `Nova Comp` = control

## Sonic Character
Nova Comp should sound:

- smooth and musical at low-to-medium settings
- gently warm as compression increases
- forgiving rather than clinical
- strong enough to control peaks when needed
- polished and finished quickly

Key internal behaviors:

- **program-dependent release** for natural movement
- **optical-style leveling feel** rather than static VCA behavior
- **subtle harmonic enhancement** as gain reduction increases
- **Magic mode** for smoother release, slight presence lift, and polished forwardness

## Mode Intent
### Smooth
Classic optical leveling feel. Soft, transparent, and vocal-friendly.

### Punch
Faster and tighter for drums, instruments, and modern control.

### Limit
Higher ratio and stronger peak control for assertive leveling and bus restraint.

## Main Use Cases
- **Vocals:** instant forwardness and stable level
- **Bass:** thick, even sustain
- **Drums:** punch and glue
- **Master / Bus:** subtle control without obvious pumping

## Visual Direction
Nova Comp should use a **cooler gold / neutral cream** palette so it feels distinct from the warmer copper energy of `Nova Heat` and the champagne tone of `Nova Tone`.

Visual cues:
- premium cream faceplate
- brushed gold accents
- elegant VU meter at the right
- minimal but luxurious control layout
- consistent Nova family branding

## Success Metric
If a user drops Nova Comp on a vocal or bass track and gets a better sound within seconds, the plugin is doing its job.
